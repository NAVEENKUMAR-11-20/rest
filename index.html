Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Driven by Fire

"Food is not just nourishment — it is memory, culture, and the warmth of a flame shared between people."

Ember & Ash was born from a singular obsession: what happens when premium ingredients meet the ancient alchemy of fire? Founded in 2012 by Chef Marcus Solène, our kitchen is a laboratory of wood-fired craft — every cut aged, every sauce reduced with patience, every plate a canvas of controlled intensity. We source exclusively from local farms within 80 miles, honoring the land that feeds us.

Behind the Fire

Chef
Marcus
Solène

Trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens across Paris, Tokyo, and New York, Marcus returned home to create something deeply personal. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, trust the fire, and let the season lead. Every menu at Ember & Ash changes with the harvest, never the trend.
